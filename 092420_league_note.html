SEPT 24 2020

Raising the bar

The Crackers, the all-time winningest team, have set a new record high score with their 161.42 win over the Afternoon Delights in Week 2. The Delights held the previous high score, at 160.08, from a 2015 Week 3 win over the Hulkamaniacs. The Crackers were led by Aaron Jones with 3-TD and 200+ yards. Matty "Ice" Ryan was also good in an embarassing loss to the Dallas Cowboys. Team Uncle Bill had an impressive week, taking down the Crusaders 156.9-122.54. Team Uncle Bill's score is the 6th most of all time. Dak Prescott, known for his garbage time stat padding, accidentally fell into a comeback win when the Falcons onsides recovery team gave the game away. The Crusaders managed to be the 3rd highest scoring team this week in their loss, which puts them at 0-2. Since there's no Bad Beat winner this week, the prize pool increases to $30.

Team America took down the Hulkamaniacs, winning by 10. Team America has both Aaron Rodgers and Russell Wilson rostered, respectively the 6th and 1st highest scoring QBs so far this year. Alvin Kamara's 2-TD, 174 yard game wasn't enough for the Hulkamaniacs. Hall easily beat the Street Fighters, but lost his first round pick, Saquon Barkley, in the process. Chris Carson is a strong second option at RB, but the injury will likely push Brandin Cooks into a starting FLEX position for Hall. The Street Fighters started two players who ended their games with big, fat ZEROs! Though active, Will Fuller the fif was not targeted at all. In Tampa, Gronk is struggling to reconnect with his former lover, Tom Brady. Gronk was targeted just once, on a throw that resulted in an interception.

The Ed Horne Football Team recorded their first win as a franchise by beating The Bareback Barback 122.4-107.08. Tommy's all-time record sits at 71-87, good for worst winning percentage of an active team (RamRod and Fuzzman are worse). EHFT was led by Tyler Higbee's 3 TDs on the Eagles "defense". Tommy got only 10 points from his QB postion. Farmer Fran managed a win with only 93.56, thanks to Waiver Wire's abysmal 64.58 score. Christian McCaffrey led the way for Fran, but will miss several weeks with an injury. Wire may need to hit the waivers to find a new QB if Carson Wentz doesn't improve. Greg Olsen, who put up a ZERO on one target, is also struggling, though he scored 10.4 in week 1.

Eagles suprised to learn last two games weren't the preseason

The Eagles sucked on offense and defense last week. They'll need to improve in both areas to be a contender this year. Fortunately, they play the Bengals this week, a team much better at sucking than the Eagles. Though the Bengals have sucked for over a decade, the Eagles have not beaten the Bengals since 2000! Bill Clinton was still getting blown in the oval office back then!
