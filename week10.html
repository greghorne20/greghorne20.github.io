Playoff Odds Analysis

The Crusaders requested that the league office perform playoff odds analysis. Later, the Crusaders declared analytics to be "bullshit".

Click image to enlarge

Hall, Farmer Fran, and Team Uncle Bill appear to be the class of the league this year. Fran and Uncle Bill face off this week in a game that will have major implications in determining which teams get a bye. Alvin Kamara returns for Fran this week and possible NFL MVP Lamar Jackson faces an awful pass defense in Cincinnati. For Team Uncle Bill, possible fantasy MVP Christian McCaffery faces an average defense in Green Bay. ESPN currently projects Uncle Bill to win by 14, but that will likely change when George Kittle's status is more certain.

Hall faces off against the Steet Fighters, whose 5-4 record is misleading. Hall likely comes away with a win. Hall does not face another team with a current winning record for the rest of the season.

Team America takes on Bareback Barback. Team America is barely alive in the playoff race, and a loss for Bareback would be a significant blow to their playoff hopes.

The monstars take on the Crusaders in a similar game, though the monstars likely have a much better chance of making the playoffs than Barback. The Crusaders cling to hope based on the fact that the Waiver Wire team was trash last year and won the championship.

The Crackers would like a win over Waiver Wire to bolster their playoff chances. At 6-3, Wire is almost guarenteed a playoff berth, but unlikely to secure a bye.

The Hulkamaniacs should take care of the Delights to keep their playoff hopes alive. Hulkamaniacs have games remaining against both Hall and the Crusaders. At least one of those teams will be tough to beat.

Fight for Tommy's $10

Tommy is the only owner to be fined so far under the new league rules. The fines go to the team with the most points against. Currently, Team America leads, but the Crusaders, Delights, and Barback are close.
