COVID-19 DUES AND PRIZES LEAGUE NEWS DRAFT BOARD LEAGUE
NOV 11 2020

Week 9

The Afternoon Delights win the high score prize for the second time. They also lead in the pool to win all the Tommy fines with a 100 point lead over the next closest team in Points Against. $20 will be added to that pot thanks to a David Johnson move to IR (Hulkamanaics - 2nd fine) and another namesake infraction (his second). Tommy has left George Kittle starting for two weeks in a row, after being placed on IR back on November 5. The Afternoon Delights are eagerly waiting to see if he starts again this week for Barback. No one has won the Bad Beat in a while. It's up to $105 for the final week. If it's not hit the money goes back into the Winner/Runner-up pool.

Week 11 could be a wild week for some teams vying for the playoffs. Team Uncle bill has clinched the #1 seed by virtue of a 2 game lead over the next best team. The Crusaders have opted to take themselves out of the running for the playoffs to give other teams a chance. The Crusaders are likely to finish the season with the 2nd most points scored. There are 3 available playoff slots for 5 teams. Hall, the Crackers, and the Ed Horne Football team are in a dead heat. Waiver Wire has a leg up on the competition, and Team America is right there, but has a big tiebreaker disadvantage. These are the playoff scenarios from the perspective of each team:

Waiver Wire

It would take an act of God for Waiver Wire to miss the playoffs. For that to happen,

  • Hall would need to beat Wire by at least 14.72 AND
  • Crackers vs Ed Horne Football: If the Crackers win, they'd need to outscore Wire by 28.44; If EFT wins, they'd need to outscore Wire by 32.1 AND
  • Team America would need to beat Team Uncle Bill and outscore Wire by 101.12
This occured in 17 out of 100,000 simulations by the forecaster, or a 0.017% chance.

Team America

They almost need to beat the 1st place Uncle Bill to have a chance this week. If they lose, they'd need:

  • A Hall Loss AND
  • Outscore both Hall and the loser of Crackers vs EFT by 70+ points (in a loss themselves)
That's extremely unlikely, but not completely impossible. With a win Team America would only need:
  • A Hall loss OR
  • A Hall win AND Team America would need to make up 70+ points on one of three teams: Hall, Waiver Wire, or the winner of EFT vs Crackers.
By far the easiest path is a Team America win and a Hall loss.

Hall

A win almost assures a playoff berth. With a win they only need:

  • Win by 14.72 over Waiver Wire OR
  • A Team America loss OR
  • Waiver Wire to lose by less than 14.72 AND a Team America win AND Team America to outscore them by 86.4 AND the winner of Crackers/EFT to outscore them by 13.72 or 17.38

If Hall loses, they'd need:

  • A Team America loss AND
  • To NOT be outscored by 86.4 by Team America AND
  • To NOT be outscored by 13.72 by a losing Crackers team or 17.38 by a losing Ed Horne Football Team

Crackers

Nearly a play-in game. The only scenario where they win and MISS the playoffs is

  • Hall wins AND
  • Team America wins AND
  • Team America outscores the Crackers by 72.68 AND
  • The Crackers DO NOT outscore either Hall by 13.72 or Waiver Wire by 28.44.

If the Crackers lose, then the only way they make the playoffs is:

  • Hall loses AND
  • Crackers outscore Hall by 13.72 AND
  • Team America loses AND
  • Team America does not outscore the Crackers by 72.68

Ed Horne Football Team

Nearly a play-in game. The only scenario where they win and MISS the playoffs is

  • Hall wins AND
  • Team America wins AND
  • Team America outscores EFT by 69.02 AND
  • EFT DOES NOT outscore either Hall by 17.38 or Waiver Wire by 32.1.

If EFT loses, then the only way they make the playoffs is:

  • Hall loses AND
  • EFT outscores Hall by 17.38 AND
  • Team America loses AND
  • Team America does not outscore EFT by 69.02

Summary

Waiver Wire is in great shape. Hall, the Crackers, and Ed Horne Football Team are in great shape if they win. Hall has a chance if he loses. The Crackers and Ed Horne need a lot of help if they lose. Team America needs a win and some help or a loss and a miracle. These scenarios can also be seen below in a chart made by Hall.
