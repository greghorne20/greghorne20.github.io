NOV 11 2020

Week 9

Team Uncle Bill has virtually clinched a top 2 spot and Waiver Wire has nearly clinched a playoff berth. Behind them, Hall and the Crackers are the overwhelming favorites to make the playoffs, but are not locked in. Win out and they are in.

The Ed Horne Football Team is against the ropes, likely needing to win out to get in. Their most straightforward path to the playoffs is to win out, including the Week 11 game against the Crackers. They're in a tight race with the Crackers in the points tiebreaker (Crackers currently lead by 28) and would make up some of that ground in a win against them. Hall is slightly farther ahead (46 points), but can be caught by EFT in a few different scenarios. There's a lot of uncertainty remaining in the possible outcomes for EFT.

Team America is in a very tough spot. Thanks to their deficit in the points tiebreaker, they're essentially 2 games back of Hall and the Crackers, and 1 game back of EFT. They are OUT with a loss this week and an EFT win.

The Crusaders (2nd in points on the season), are in better position than Team America, but need some help. They will need to beat the Street Fighters (2-7) this week and lame-duck champion Farmer Fran (3-6) in Week 11. Assuming those two things happen, the Crusader's playoff chances forecast jumps to 26%. Then the Crusaders need either Hall or the Crackers to lose out, EFT to lose at least one, and Team America to lose at least one. (Or both Hall and Crackers lose out, and one of EFT/America to lose at least one. Hall faces the tougher remaining schedule of the two 6-win teams (according to the forecast).

The five remaining teams have been eliminated, but are suing ESPN, the NFL, the fantasy league, Obama, and PETA, hoping to get the previous game's scores recounted or something.
