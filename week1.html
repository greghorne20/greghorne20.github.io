Eli Manning Hall Of Fame Candidacy Update

Case for Eli Manning getting into the Hall of Fame

  • Eli Manning was the starting quarterback for 2 super bowl winning teams
  • Eli has been very healthy over his career, starting every game from mid-2004 to his 2017 benching, so he has piled up impressive cumulative stats. Coaches who have benched him for sucking were fired.

Case against Eli Manning getting into the Hall of Fame

All stats are from 2004-now (his career), for players with 48+ starts (53 total)

  • Eli Manning has never been a first or second team all-pro.
  • Eli Manning is 37th in completion percentage, just below Mcnabb and above Fitzpatrick
  • Eli Manning is 38th best in Interception-% just above Jameis Winston and Joey Harrington.
  • Eli is 32nd in QB rating, just below Joe Flacco.
  • Eli is 24th in Adjust Net Yards per Attempt, right above Chad Pennington.
  • Eli is 2 games above .500 on his career W/L, at a .512 winning percentage. That is worse than the Phillies this year (for now).
  • Eli will be below .500 after this year, unless he gets benched early.
  • Eli led the Giants to losing or 8-8 seasons in 2004 (his rookie season, he went 1-6 after Warner went 5-4), 2006, 2009, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2017, & 2018).
