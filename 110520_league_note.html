Week 9

We are 8 weeks into the season and there have been 8 different teams with the high score of the week. That's a bit unexpected. At 7-1 and with a solid lead in points, Team Uncle Bill has nearly locked up 1st place. Barksdale's Waiver Wire had an important win over the Crusaders to make them the favorite for 2nd place. The Ed Horne Football Team had a very impressive win over Hall, winning 62.0 to 61.06. Both of those teams are more likely than the Crusaders to make the playoffs. The Crackers were right up there with them, at 60.16 points in a loss to the Cracker Sr. The Crackers are also more likely to make the playoffs than the Crusaders.

Team America jumped from 11% to 25% to make the playoffs with a win over the Street Fighters in which they scored over 100. The Crusaders, second in total points for the season are down to a 39% chance of making the playoffs, and that's with an expected win rate of 67% over the average competition and 2 of their final 3 games against the 11th and 12th placed teams. If the Crusaders miss the playoffs, they are expected to refuse to accept the results, use their power as commisioner to spread misinformation, sue everyone for anything, and rant about the rampant fantasy fraud that took place. Stop counting the scores! Or count all the scores, I'm not sure.

Barback Bareback and the Hulkamaniacs still have a very outside shot at the playoffs. The Delights are toast. The reigning champion Farmer Fran sucks. And the Street Fighters are awful.

The league office HQ is in a transitionary phase, having moved into a temporary location, but expect to move into new fancier offices in time for the playoffs. No bad beat this week; The pool is up to $75 for week 9.
